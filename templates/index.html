TATA ACE

The standard Ace HT is powered by a two-cylinder 702 cc engine, delivering 16 hp (12 kW) at 3200 rpm and a torque of 3.8 kgf⋅m (37 N⋅m) at 2000 rpm.

It has a permissible loading capacity of 750 kg (1650 lb).

It is equipped with a four-speed manual gearbox and has a top speed of 60 km/h (37 mph).

The Ace HT has a modern cabin compared to its three-wheeler rivals. It is best suited for carrying goods over short distances.

The Ace EX adds a five-speed gearbox, stop-start and larger wheels (13" over 12") and wider tires (155 rather than 145), and a top speed of 70 km/h.
